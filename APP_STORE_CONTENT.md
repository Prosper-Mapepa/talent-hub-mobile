## Description (4,000 characters max)

CMU TalentHub connects Carnegie Mellon University students with career opportunities, peer collaborations, and businesses. Think LinkedIn meets Upwork, built exclusively for the CMU community.

**FOR STUDENTS:**

• Showcase Your Talents: Create professional profiles with rich media portfolios. Upload videos, images, code repositories, and project demonstrations.

• Discover Opportunities: Browse and apply for internships, jobs, and project collaborations. Filter by industry, location, skills, and compensation.

• Build Your Network: Connect with peers, faculty, alumni, and businesses in a verified, secure environment.

• Get Discovered: Let recruiters and faculty find you through our advanced search system.

• Track Applications: Monitor job applications with status tracking and interview scheduling.

• Real-Time Messaging: Communicate directly with employers, collaborators, and mentors.

**FOR BUSINESSES & FACULTY:**

• Access Premium Talent: Browse verified student profiles with portfolios showcasing skills, projects, and achievements.

• Post Opportunities: Create and manage job listings, internships, and collaborations. Track applications from your dashboard.

• Find the Right Fit: Use advanced search filters for skills, majors, experience levels, and portfolios.

• Direct Communication: Connect instantly with students. Screen candidates and schedule interviews.

• Application Management: Review and manage applications from your dedicated dashboard.

**KEY FEATURES:**

✅ Verified Community: CMU email verification ensures authentic community members

✅ Portfolio-First Design: Showcase student work with videos, images, and code samples

✅ Comprehensive Profiles: Detailed profiles with skills, achievements, portfolios, and experience

✅ Advanced Search: Powerful filters for skills, majors, experience level, and location

✅ Real-Time Messaging: Built-in messaging for seamless communication

✅ Job Management: Complete job posting with application tracking and candidate management

✅ Mobile-Optimized: Beautiful iOS interface with smooth navigation

✅ Secure & Private: Industry-standard security with visibility controls

✅ Notifications: Real-time alerts for opportunities, messages, and updates

✅ Analytics: Track profile views, applications, and engagement metrics
